AI-Learner Buddy

Project Overview

This platform is an AI-powered Course Generator that helps learners and educators instantly create structured learning materials. Powered by Google Gemini AI, it generates course outlines, chapters, and interactive explanations based on your chosen topic & difficulty level.

> Purpose

  • Save time in designing study plans & course structures.
  • Provide beginner, intermediate, and advanced-level courses instantly.
  • Make education more accessible, adaptive, and engaging with AI.

> How It Works

  1. Register or log in to your account.
  2. Enter a topic, number of chapters, and level.
  3. AI generates a structured course outline.
  4. Click a chapter to generate detailed content in Markdown.
  5. Content is stored in your dashboard for easy access later.
